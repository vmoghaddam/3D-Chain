Know 3D-Chain

3D-Chain was founded in Los Angeles, CA in November 2017.

3D-Chain connects people with local and international manufacturers from 3D Printing and Laser Cutting to CNC Machining.

3D-Chain develops around the connections made between Manufacturers, Designers, and Consumers.

Manufacturer or designer can set up an account (webpage) to post information, photos, and price about their products.

Consumers will receive instant pricing and feed back from manufacturers.

Consumers order parts or designs and write reviews.

Consumers can review manufacturers recommendations and requirements before uploading their design.

3D-Chain uses automated software to recommend the most helpful and reliable reviews to the 3D-Chain community.

3D-Chain’s newsletter brings updates on the latest manufacturers, designers and R&D.

3D-Chain’s R&D network support dynamic growth along technical or managerial pathways.
